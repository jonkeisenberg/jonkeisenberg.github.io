Jon Eisenberg

I'm senior director of the Computer Science and Telecommunications Board at the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine. Prior to joining the Academies, I was a AAAS Science, Engineering, and Diplomacy Fellow at the U.S. Agency for International Development. I earned a Ph.D. in experimental high-energy Physics from the University of Washington.

I study computing research impacts and needs, responsible computing research, societal impacts and policy implications of computing, research cyberinfrastructure, cybersecurity, defense software and acquisition, health IT, emergency management, and government IT modernization.
